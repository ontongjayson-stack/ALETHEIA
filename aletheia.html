Why ALETHEIA Exists

Throughout history, powerful institutions have suppressed technologies that threatened their control. The water-powered engine. The free energy device. The cure that never made it to market. The inventor who disappeared.

They could kill a person. They could buy a patent. They could burn a lab. But they could not kill an idea shared with everyone, simultaneously, permanently, on a network no one controls.

ALETHEIA is built on a simple belief: truth, once released, cannot be recaptured.

We are not anti-science. We are not conspiracy theorists. We are archivists. We preserve what others try to erase, and we trust humanity to decide what is real.

Every schematic published here is hashed to the blockchain. Every blueprint is permanent. Every inventor is protected by mathematics, not law — because law can be purchased, but the chain cannot.

01 // PERMANENCE
Once published, always published
All verified submissions are written to Arweave — a permanent, decentralized storage network. No server can be seized. No domain can be taken down. The data exists as long as the network exists.
02 // OPENNESS
Free to access, free to build
Every blueprint, schematic, and instruction set is freely accessible to every human on earth. No paywalls. No gatekeepers. Knowledge belongs to everyone.
03 // PROTECTION
Moderation without censorship
A decentralized council of moderators reviews submissions for quality and safety before publication. Content is filtered for harm, not for inconvenience to the powerful.
04 // TRUTH
ἀλήθεια — unconcealedness
The ancient Greek concept of truth as that which cannot remain hidden. That is what ALETHEIA does. It unconceals. It reveals. It publishes what was buried.
